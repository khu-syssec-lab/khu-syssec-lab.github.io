Welcome to the System Security Lab (KHU-SysSec)! We are part of School of Computing and Graduate School of Security Convergence at Kyung Hee University. Our mission is to explore the real-world security issues in 4G/5G cellular networks and wireless communication across various innovative systems such as smartphones, unmanned vehicles, and satellites.

We are seeking self-motivated and passionate graduate & under-graduate students with keen interest in wireless security. If you are interested, contact us or see the datails of join our lab.
